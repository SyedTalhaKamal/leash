OUR SERVICES!

We offer an ongoing dogwalking service where we require a three walk minimum per week on an ongoing basis with consistent set days and pick up times.

BUDDY WALKS

Buddy walks are excellent for dog owners who are flexible and open to pairing their pup with a walking mate. These walks are alsoperfect for dogs who love to socialize with other dogs.and want a compatible walking ...buddy.

READ MORE

PRIVATE WALKS

Our private dog walks allow our walkers to give your pup special one-on-one attention. These walks are excellent for pups with behavorial or health issues that require them to be limited toprivate walks only. These walks offer adequate time for relief, excercise, and bonding.

READ MORE

PUPPY VISITS

Our puppy care visits provide your pup with individualized attention and special care. These visits can be both indoor/outdoor and tailored to your specific needs including: feedings; changing pads; playtime, and lots of love.!

READ MORE

SIBLING WALKS

Our sibling walks are for dog owners who have more than one dog. These walks offer you a discounted rate and offer your pups adequate time for relief, excercise, and bonding. Sibling walking rates are limited to two dogs

READ MORE

ADDITIONAL SERVICES

Other services that can be provided in lieu of your regular scheduled services include: vet/groomer pick ups or drop offs, meetings with dog trainers.

READ MORE

RATES & HOURS

Rates do not include nyc sales tax of 8.875%. Hours of operation are Monday – Friday, from 9:00 A.M – 5:00 P.M. Walks starting before 9:00 AM or after 5:00 PM are $6 more than regular hour walks. Weekend and holiday walks... can be arranged based on walker availability, however they are an additional $10 per service. We observe the U.S. Federal Holiday Calendar.
